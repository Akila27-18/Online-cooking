FAQ's

1. How do I find a recipe on your website?
You can easily find a recipe by using the search bar at the top of the page. Simply type in the dish you're looking for, and we'll provide a list of relevant recipes. Alternatively, you can browse recipes by category (e.g., Quick Meals, Desserts, Vegan, etc.).
2. Can I save my favorite recipes?
Yes, if you create an account, you can save your favorite recipes to your profile for easy access later.
3. How do I submit my own recipes?
We'd love to see your recipes! You can submit your recipes through the 'Submit Recipe' link in the footer.
4. Are the recipes suitable for beginners?
Yes, many of our recipes are designed with beginners in mind. We also have a dedicated section for simple, step-by-step recipes.
5. How do I know if a recipe is vegan, gluten-free, or dairy-free?
Every recipe page includes tags and dietary labels that specify if the dish is vegan, gluten-free, or dairy-free.
